A slice of lemon just doesn't cut it for us

We don’t do things like other gin makers. Since 2009, Caorunn Gin has been handcrafted in small batches at Balmenach Distillery in the Scottish Highlands. Our unique blend of 5 handpicked Celtic botanicals & 6 classic gin botanicals are distilled in the world's only working Copper Berry Chamber to extract maximum flavour. That’s not just ingenious, that’s Gin Genius.
